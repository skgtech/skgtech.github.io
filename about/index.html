About SKGTech

who we are

We are SKGTech.

SKGTech is a non profit organization based in Thessaloniki, Greece. The organization is comprised of creative and passionate people that love all the things revolving around technology.

We organize local meetups, international conferences and create apps and services to aid our society at large. SKGTech is all about people; networking and bringing the people with common interests together to create synergies.
